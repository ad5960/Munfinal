About Us

SRM Model United Nations Society is a youth organisation established in 2011. Ever since, it has created a legacy and made a mark on the Indian MUN circuit. Over the decade, we have had tens of thousands of students from across the country take part in our flagship event, The SRM Model United Nations Conference - a living embodiment of hard work and success. Society demands for change and our organisation is a reflection of that change. At the onset of this new decade, SRM MUN transformed into the SRM MUN Society to expand its horizon and encompass a larger spectrum. The SRM MUNSoc is an establishment responsible for supervising students albeit nurturing competent individuals, with a dexterity for organising, and most importantly offering a space for MUN aficionados to learn and network with others. The Society comprises two parallel branches- the Organising Committee and the Delegation. The Organising Committee is responsible for facilitating the working of all activities carried out within the organisation. It is further categorised into six specialised domains - Business Relations & Marketing, Delegate Affairs & Hospitality, Design, Operations, Tech, and Public Relations. The Delegation serves as the lynchpin of SRM MUNSoc and takes part primarily in MUN conferences across the country. This year, it has evolved to be an epicentre that encourages and trains individuals in any form of public speaking, debate, art, writing, quizzes, journalism, and photojournalism, in addition to the MUN conferences. Together, the Organising Committee and Delegation, headed by the Secretariat, over 200 members strong, spearhead the organisation of collegiate events and initiatives, while our teams achieve glory at renowned regional and national level competitive events.
